This passion enhances my work in beauty and music journalism, enabling me to craft compelling narratives and foster meaningful connections between artists and audiences. My Experience in Beauty

In my role as a Japanese beauty and music journalist and PR professional, I have amassed extensive experience in the beauty industry. I have worked on high-profile beauty campaigns and collaborated with leading brands to innovate and elevate beauty standards. My expertise in beauty journalism allows me to provide in-depth analysis and insights, while my PR skills help me effectively communicate and promote beauty trends and products to a wide audience.

Ambassador in Digital Media

As a Japanese-Swedish digital content ambassador, my role uniquely combines my experience as a Japanese translator and ambassador for Swedish television (SVT) with my expertise as a digital marketing lead for a Swedish video game company entering the Japanese market. My work has involved crafting successful campaigns and strategic marketing efforts, using my dual cultural insights to bridge Japanese and Swedish communities. This has enhanced game visibility, fostered strong connections between developers and players, and driven engagement and growth within the gaming industry.
